## Hours of Operation

**Most County Offices**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

**Exceptions**:
- Resource Management counter: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM (closed 12-1 PM)
- Tax Collector: Extended hours during tax deadlines
- Library branches: Varies by location

**County Holidays**: New Year's, MLK Day, Presidents Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Veterans Day, Thanksgiving (2 days), Christmas Eve, Christmas

## Tips for Calling

1. **Have your APN ready** - Most property questions need it
2. **Know your address** - Including whether city or unincorporated
3. **Describe your project** - What are you trying to do?
4. **Ask about forms** - Many applications are online
5. **Request a callback** - If wait times are long
